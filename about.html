About KACST

"To be a world-class science and technology organization that fosters innovation and promotes knowledge-based society in the Kingdom."
Vision of KACST

"To develop and invest in the national science, technology, and innovation eco-system to promote knowledge-based society that serves the Kingdom’s sustainable development, through: • Formulating national policies and plans for science, technology, and innovation • Coordinating science, technology, and innovation activities nationally • Conducting applied research and technology development"
KACST Mission

"To develop and invest in the national science, technology, and innovation eco-system to promote knowledge-based society that serves the Kingdom’s sustainable development, through: • Providing support to scientific research and technology development in the kingdom • Investing in technology development and commercialization • Establishing and fostering local, regional, and international cooperation and partnerships for technology transfer, localization, and development"
